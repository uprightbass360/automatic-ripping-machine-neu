Where should the gear menu live on the drive card?

The gear icon opens a settings panel for per-drive options like rip speed. Here are three approaches:

A

Gear icon in the action bar

Add a small gear icon at the right end of the existing action bar (next to Eject/Insert/Scan). Clicking it expands a settings panel below the action bar.

Auto | Eject Insert Scan ⚙
↓ Settings panel expands here (rip speed, future settings)
B

Gear icon in the card header

Place the gear icon in the top-right corner of the card (next to the status badge). Clicking it toggles a settings section at the bottom of the card.

C

Dedicated settings row below the action bar

Always show a small settings row beneath the action bar. No gear icon needed - the rip speed selector is just always visible as a compact row.
